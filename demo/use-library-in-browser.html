Try copying the following into your browser console (Ctrl+Shift+I to open dev tools in Chrome and FireFox) 
response=await fetch('/data/iris.data');
stringData=await response.text();
data=parseCsv(stringData, new IrisRowHandler()).result;
lossFn=new BinaryCrossEntropyLoss();
model=[new Linear(4,50), new ReLU(), new Linear(50,3), new Sigmoid()];
learn=new Learner(model, lossFn, data);
learn.fit(25);
